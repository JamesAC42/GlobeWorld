Mission Statement

Globe World strives to provide our customers with only the finest and most novel range of products, making your stay here in New Mexico a trip to remember. Our unique product line is the hallmark of our brand, and failure to satisfy will result in 100% reimbursement

History

Globe World began as an atlas dealer and travel guide in Vermont 2014 (think AAA), created by tycoon James Crovo. They moved to Teec Nos Pos, New Mexico and transitioned its product line to novelty items. This attracted the tourist population near the Four Corners Monument.
